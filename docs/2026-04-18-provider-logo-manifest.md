## Legal note
Brand logos used purely to denote the respective OAuth/OIDC provider that the login
button dispatches to. Not modified beyond single-color simplification. For production,
confirm each provider's brand-usage guidelines; SimpleIcons aggregates them but individual
brands may have stricter visual requirements.
